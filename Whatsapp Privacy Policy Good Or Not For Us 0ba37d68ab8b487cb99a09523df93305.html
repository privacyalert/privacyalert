Whatsapp Privacy Policy Good Or Not For Us.

What Information Is At Risk

  • Account Information Such As Profile Picture, About, Status like (Hey There, I'm using whatsapp)
  • Whatsapp Says That Our Messages Are End To End Encrypted But They Store Messages Temporarily. So Messages Also At Risk.
  • The Media You Forward Is Also Saved Temporarily To Their Servers.
  • Contacts In Your Phone.
  • Transactions And Payments Data (After Launch Of New Feature Whatsapp Payment).
  • Customer Support Chats And Communications.
  • Device And Connection Information.
  • Location Of Specific User And Phone.
  • Cookies.

How They Will Use This Information

  1. They will work with third-party service providers and other Facebook Companies to help us operate, provide, improve, understand, customize, support, and market our Services.
  1. They enable you and third parties, like businesses, to communicate and interact with each other using their services, such as Catalogs for businesses on WhatsApp through which you can browse products and services and place orders.
  1. They will share your contact details to third party service providers.

So Should We Use Whatsapp Now?

No We Should Not.
There is Alternative App Out There Like Whatsapp. Named "Signal Private Messenger". They Claim That They Take Care Of These Privacy Matters.
So Many People Including Elon Musk Said To Use "Signal Private Messenger".
As We Know, Mark Zuckerberg Founder Of Facebook Bought Whatsapp in 2014. We Should Not Support Privacy Breachers.
